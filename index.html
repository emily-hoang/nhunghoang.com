The global Bahmni Project

During the pandemic 2019, I contributed my time to develop new features which supports the Bahmni - an easy-to-use EMR & hospital system.

It combines and enhances existing open source products(OpenMRS, OpenELIS, Odoo & dcm4chee) into a single solution.

It is one of the most popular open-source EMR & HMIS products, with 500+ downloads per month.

Bahmni is a strategic asset to many humanitarian organizations and recommended EMR/EHR by Ministries of Health of countries like Tanzania, Bangladesh, and Rwanda.

It is one of the most popular open-source software for low resource settings in the world.

It has been implemented in 50+ countries and more than 400+ implementations (e.g. Lesotho alone has 180 implementations).

For COVID-19, Bahmni has been re-purposed as a ready-to-use system for:

  • COVID19 related testing, managing patient/individual information. E.g: travelling.
  • Share information with relevant Authorities and systems in a secure and safe manner.
  • Record health information for Covid19 patients.
  • Manage patient treatments, progress, and follow-ups.
  • Helps public health researchers to get insights from management through:
  • Ability to arrange and optimize the use of resources as PPE kits, ventilators.
  • Ability to detect patterns of communicable diseases and inform policymakers to take pre-emptive action in preventing contagions.
  • Supports patients and doctors to join a video call via a free, light-weight application.
